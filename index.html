Sothis Acupuncture
Ancient wisdom creating harmony

Where physical and emotional healing begins.

With over 3000 years of development and wisdom of experience accumulated, acupuncture has proven the efficacy in the treatment of a wide range of conditions.

The human body is a microcosm of the earth, just like the earth we are 70% water, we have paths where vital energy flows. On earth the roads are rivers that carry water to ourish the earth, in the body are meridians where vital energy ir Qi flows.

When the pathways are blocked by physical or emotional trauma, the flow of Qi is blocked leading to disharmomy or dysfunction in the body.

Acupuncture restores flow in the blockages by stimulating body walls such as skin, muscles, and the web of connective tissue that wraps around all internal parts of the body from head to toe to produce a therapeutic effect
