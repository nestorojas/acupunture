Ancient Wisdom Creating Harmony

Where physical and emotional healing begins.

With over 3,000 years of development and wisdom, acupuncture has proven its efficacy in treating a wide range of conditions.

The human body is a microcosm of the earth. Like the earth, we are 70% water and have paths where vital energy flows. On Earth, the roads are rivers that carry water to nourish the land. In the body, these are meridians where vital energy, or Qi, flows.

When these pathways are blocked by physical or emotional trauma, the flow of Qi is disrupted, leading to disharmony or dysfunction in the body.

Acupuncture restores the flow in these blockages by stimulating body areas such as the skin, muscles, and the web of connective tissue that wraps around all internal parts of the body from head to toe to produce a therapeutic effect.
